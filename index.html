A Private Coastal Sanctuary

NOD offers an intimate escape with just six meticulously designed seafront suites, each featuring private dipping pools and uninterrupted ocean views. Our philosophy is simple: provide unparalleled privacy, personalized service, and moments of pure tranquility.

At NOD, every detail is curated to create an experience that resonates with your soul. From the architecture that harmonizes with the coastal landscape to the bespoke amenities that anticipate your every need.

Culinary Excellence

Our culinary philosophy celebrates the bounty of the sea and local terroir. Each dish tells a story of place and season, crafted by our talented chefs who transform the freshest ingredients into unforgettable culinary experiences.

From intimate beachfront dinners to private chef experiences in your suite, every meal at NOD is a celebration of flavor, artistry, and connection.

Wellness & Spa

Our wellness philosophy draws inspiration from the healing power of the sea. Each treatment is designed to restore balance and harmony, using marine-based ingredients and ancient techniques adapted for modern luxury.

From sunrise yoga on the beach to bespoke spa journeys in private treatment rooms, NOD offers a sanctuary for complete rejuvenation.
