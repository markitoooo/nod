Private Coastal Sanctuary

NOD offers an intimate escape with just six meticulously designed seafront suites, each featuring private dipping pools and uninterrupted ocean views. Our philosophy is simple: provide unparalleled privacy, personalized service, and moments of pure tranquility.

At NOD, every detail is curated to create an experience that resonates with your soul. From the architecture that harmonizes with the coastal landscape to the bespoke amenities that anticipate your every need.
